History

Family Owned and Operated Since 1940

  • 1930s

    Origins in Flavor

    Felix Epstein began selling herbs and spices from a small storefront pharmacy in Germany, finding his niche in the sausage and meat industry.

  • 1940

    First Spice Mixing Company

    Felix Epstein immigrated to the United States and opened the First Spice Mixing Company in lower Manhattan, New York.

  • 1950s

    Rapid Expansion

    Due to overwhelming demand, First Spice expanded to Long Island City, New York, San Francisco, California, and Toronto, Canada.

  • Late 20th Century

    New Leadership

    Felix Epstein’s son Peter, with a degree in Food Science and Technology from Cornell University, brought First Spice into the twenty-first century.

  • Present Day

    Continuing Innovation

    Peter’s three daughters: Vicki, Marcy, and Wendy, are innovating First Spice with new products that continue to exceed customer expectations.

PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT

Our R&D Department offers the perfect balance of creativity and technical expertise. Our expert food technologists work directly with you in every phase of the creative process, from prototype to finished product. For over 70 years, this one-on-one relationship has led to the creation of some of the most successful products on food service tables and supermarket shelves.

At First Spice, we are an extension of your product development team. As your partner, we are able to make a seasoning blend to match an existing flavor profile or develop a completely new product to meet your exact specifications. We offer creative methods for achieving a clean label and an accurate ingredients statement without sacrificing flavor profiles, USDA, kosher and nutritional requirements.

We have three manufacturing plants, each equipped with professionally staffed laboratories. This enables each of our R&D teams to work closely with you throughout the development process. The quality, consistency and care we take in developing and preparing our products keeps First Spice on top of our competitors.

Food Safety & Quality Control


Food Safety considerations are an integral part of our processes and procedures. Our emphasis on Food Safety begins before raw materials enter our facility with strict supplier quality control programs. These Food Safety programs continue through the production and packaging of all our products. Our Food Safety programs include: HACCP, Allergen Controls, Glass & Brittle Plastics Control, Good Manufacturing Processes, Supplier Review, Environmental Monitoring, Lot Coding and Lot Tracking. As further assurance of the safety and quality of our products, First Spice is monitored during Independent Third-Party Audits.

Our extensive Quality Assurance Program guarantees food safety, quality and consistency in every order we ship and every product we receive. First Spice is committed to total Food Safety. We have a highly trained laboratory staff to ensure consistent color, flavor, aroma and taste.
